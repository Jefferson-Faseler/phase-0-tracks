Looking into a day in the life of a NY house cat

When the windows turn on and my humans have not yet stirred I must act. On a good day my humans will wake to make themselves a crushed hot bean drink, it smells of dirt and occasionally another cat's poop. But on the days when the windows turn on and they still lay there I must rub their faces or they will die.

After the humans have consumed their hot bean drink I might get scratches from my human.

On good days it continues. Ooooh, aaaaaaahh...
