Genre Characteristics Why I appreciate it
Classical Melodic emphasis, rhythms, and movements. Inspries thought and are revered for their complexity.
Rock Emphasis on electric guitar, with staples such as bass, drums, and vocals. The starting point for most modern musical creativity. It relies heavily on passionate emotions.
Jazz Heavy use of polyrhythms and improvisation make this music infinite in possiblities, and always evolving in style. Jazz can contain multiple solos and relies heavily on each member being in one mindset together. Sometimes jazz music doesn't resolve, forcing the listener to reflect and wait for more.
